Title: Plorate Filii Isræl

Lyrics

Plorate Filii IsraelWeep, Sons of IsraelPlora Filios de Israel
Plorate filii Isræl;
plorate omnes virgines;
et filiam Iephte unigenitam
in carmine doloris lamentamini. 		Weep, sons of Israel;
weep, all virgins;
and for the daughter, of Jephte the only begotten
in a song of grief, lament.
