Information

Title: Plorate Filii Isræl
Composer: G. Carissimi
Language: Latin
From: Iephte / Historia di Jephte
Year: 1648

Lyrics

Plorate Filii IsrælWeep, Sons of Israel
Plorate filii Isræl;
plorate omnes virgines;
[solo] plorate virginitatem meam
et filiam Iephte unigenitam
in carmine doloris lamentamini. 		Weep, sons of Israel;
weep, all virgins;
weep for my virginity
and for the daughter, of Jephte the only begotten
in a song of grief, lament.
